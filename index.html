Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Overview

Brewdle is a word games collection app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Guest Mode (No Account)

You can play all games without creating an account. In this mode:

  • All game progress is stored locally on your device
  • No personal data is collected or transmitted
  • No tracking or analytics

With an Account (Optional)

If you choose to create an account, we collect:

  • Email address - Used for account authentication only
  • Display name - Optional, shown on leaderboards
  • Game progress - Scores, levels completed, and statistics

How We Use Your Data

  • Sync your game progress across devices
  • Display your scores on leaderboards
  • Authenticate your account

We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

If you sign in with Google or Apple, their respective privacy policies apply to the authentication process. We only receive your email address from these services.
